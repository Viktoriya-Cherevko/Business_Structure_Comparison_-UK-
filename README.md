# Business Structure Comparison (UK)

This repository contains a practical spreadsheet designed to compare different business structures in the United Kingdom:

- Limited Company (LTD)  
- Sole Trader (ST)  
- Partnership  
- Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)  

## 📊 Purpose

This spreadsheet was created as a client-facing tool to support individuals who are in the early stages of starting a business and are unsure which legal structure to choose.

Instead of providing lengthy written explanations, this comparison offers a clear, structured overview of the key differences between business types, making communication faster and more effective.

## 🧩 What the spreadsheet includes

The file provides a side-by-side comparison across multiple criteria:

- Number of owners  
- Legal structure and responsibility  
- Limited liability  
- Business name registration  
- Privacy and disclosure requirements  
- Investment options  
- Income structure  
- Taxation  
- Ease of setup  
- Reporting requirements  
- Bookkeeping complexity  
- Ongoing accountancy costs  
- VAT and PAYE applicability
